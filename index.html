Introduction to Our Church

Gospel Of Truth Mission is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the word of God and serving our community with love and compassion.

We are more than just a church; we are a family united in faith, purpose, and love for Jesus Christ. Our mission is to create an environment where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and discover their purpose in life.

At Gospel Of Truth Mission, we believe that every person has value and worth in God's eyes. We welcome people from all walks of life to join us in worship, fellowship, and service as we journey together toward spiritual growth and community transformation.

Foundation of the Church

Gospel Of Truth Mission was founded on the solid rock of Jesus Christ, with a vision to establish a church that truly reflects the heart of God.

Our Beginning

Our church was born out of a divine vision and calling to create a sanctuary where the pure gospel could be preached, where lives could be transformed, and where community could flourish. From its inception, Gospel Of Truth Mission has been committed to maintaining the authentic Christian faith as revealed in Scripture.

Built on Biblical Principles

We stand firmly on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, with Jesus Christ himself as the cornerstone. Our church structure and governance are based on biblical principles, ensuring that everything we do aligns with God's word and will.

Growing in Faith

What started as a small gathering of believers has grown into a thriving community of faith. We continue to build upon our strong foundation, always seeking to honor God in all we do and to be a light in our community.
